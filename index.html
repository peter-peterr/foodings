About Us

In 1984, Food dot opened its doors with one goal in mind: Better Ingredients. Better Food. We knew that with quality ingredients we would create superior quality food. That goal and the promise of Better Ingredients. Better Food. remain true to this day.



Our fundamental belief is simple: take care of your people and give them the best to work with and you will make superior quality food. This is what drives us and it's why we invest more than many others in the industry in our effort to consistently deliver superior food and superior service.
